About Twist & Turn

Twist & Turn is an online cubing competition series created to provide fair, exciting and affordable competitions for cubers across India. Our mission is to build a stronger cubing community by organizing professional online competitions with transparent judging, exciting prizes and a great participant experience.

Rules & Regulations

  • ✔ Follow WCA regulations wherever applicable.
  • ✔ Fair play is mandatory.
  • ✔ No video editing allowed.
  • ✔ Camera must clearly show competitor and cube.
  • ✔ Results submitted after deadline won't be accepted.
  • ✔ Organizer's decision will be final.
