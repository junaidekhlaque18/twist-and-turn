📜 Competition Rules

📹 Camera Setup

Camera must clearly show your face, hands and cube throughout the solve.

Maximum inspection time is 15 seconds. Exceeding this may result in penalties according to the competition rules.

🎥 Recording

Every solve must be recorded in one continuous video. Editing or cuts are not allowed.

🌐 Fair Play

Any cheating, assistance or unfair advantage will lead to immediate disqualification.

📶 Internet

Participants must have a stable internet connection during Google Meet sessions.

⚖ Final Decision

The organizer's decision will be final in all disputes.
